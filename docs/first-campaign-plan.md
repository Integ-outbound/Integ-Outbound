# First Campaign Plan

## First ICP

- UK and US paid media agencies
- PPC agencies
- Google Ads agencies
- Meta ads agencies
- performance marketing agencies

## Target List

- `100` agencies total
- `1` founder, CEO, or operator contact per agency
- verified work email preferred
- no generic inboxes
- manual quality review required before lead creation

## First Batch

- `20–30` emails total
- `1` sending mailbox
- daily cap `10–20`
- campaign starts `paused`
- all drafts manually reviewed before any approval
- no autonomous AI replies

## Offer Hypothesis

Done-for-you outbound pipeline generation for agencies:

- prospect sourcing
- personalized outreach
- Gmail-based sending
- reply classification
- follow-up handling
- booked-call workflow

## Pricing Hypothesis

Founder decision needed. Evaluate these options first:

- `$500` setup plus `$1,000–$2,000` per month
- `$1,500–$3,000` monthly retainer
- qualified-meeting hybrid pricing

## Data Source Plan

- Apollo first for small targeted batches
- manual agency directories
- Clutch
- LinkedIn
- Hunter, Dropcontact, or Clay only for small verified batches if budget allows
- do not enrich `1,000+` companies upfront

## Success Metrics

- reply rate
- positive reply rate
- booked calls
- wrong-person replies
- bounce rate
- objections
- cost per valid contact
- cost per positive reply

## Do Not Do Yet

- frontend
- Gmail Pub/Sub
- autonomous AI replies
- large-scale enrichment
- massive sending
- more company imports
